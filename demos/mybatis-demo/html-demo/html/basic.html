Auto-rotation is configuring a service to automatically rotate, distribute, and activate certificates/Storage Account Keys E2E without requiring any manual step, need for deployment, or restart (zero-touch).
Auto-rotation is an enabler for fast ECR at scale, plus other benefits such as: reduced risk of outages due to certificate expiry or rotation, supports quick region deployment (7-day rollout), frees up DRI and engineering resources, eliminates human error, etc.
